Welcome to YouTube/Twitch Enhancer

Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is collected or sent anywhere.

The essentials

1

Block a channel

Right-click any video tile and choose Block this YouTube channel — or open the video's ⋮ three-dot menu and pick the injected Block channel item. Every upload from that channel disappears from every page. Misclicked? Hit Undo on the toast.

2

Hide a single video

Right-click it and choose Hide this video, or Ctrl + right-click for an instant hide — that also works on end-screen suggestions over the player.

3

Block by title keywords

In Manage (toolbar button → ⚙), add words or /regex/ patterns — any video whose title matches is hidden, whoever uploaded it.

4

Shorts & watched videos

Shorts (shelves, tabs, the sidebar entry) are removed and /shorts links open in the normal player. Videos you've already watched past a threshold are hidden too — you can choose which surfaces that applies to (Home, Subscriptions, search…).

5

See what it's doing

Flip on Reveal hidden (audit) in the popup to show everything the extension filtered, dimmed with a red outline, instead of removed. The master On switch in the popup pauses the whole extension instantly.

6

Take it with you

Export/import your block list as JSON from the popup or Manage page, or enable browser sync so channels, videos and keywords follow your browser's own sync account.

7

Twitch too

On twitch.tv, right-click any stream card to block the channel or its whole category. Channel points are auto-claimed (even in background tabs), the front-page carousel is gone, and the player gains ✂ Clip / ➤ Share clip buttons plus source-quality pinning. The popup's Twitch tab holds the toggles; ⚙ Twitch advanced… opens the full Twitch manager (tags, chat filters, player tools, and more).
